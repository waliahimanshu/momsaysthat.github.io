Since last one year, we had been getting two free movie tickets each week from Vitality health insurance which of-course most of the time went unused. But when this year, when we renewed the insurance, Vitality came up with a new plan - They introduced this concept of earning 10 points through physical exercise in order yo avail the tickets each week. Shouldn’t have had been a problem since Him and I both are quite active. 

So this fine weekend, we decided to avail the point to go and watch Bajrangi Bhaijaan. When we checked my points - I had only 5 of them which meant one of us either had to buy the ticket or had to run and earn the points. So, Him decided to go out for a run - what he did’t know was he had to run only 3 miles. So in order to make sure that he gets the free ticket, he ran 10 miles! yes - 10 miles! Although, I must say I am impressed with his speed, since he managed to run 10 miles in 50 minutes. 

When he came back home from the run and checked the point status - He alone had 15 points! All came out of his mouth was - Oh! I ran a bit too much.

So dear Vitality, I am not really sure about your plan to get us moving because - 
a. Not everyone is sitting on couch, and some of us exercise regularly just not the way your app could keep a track of.
b. Your mobile app sucks, BIGTIME.

Lastly, well played Vitality. Well played.
