I am disgusted! Absolutely disgusted! I will try my best to vent explain here what pissed me off so badly.

Firstly, I am not a kind of person who judges someone. I literally never ever judge anyone for his/her choices, well unless the person is mean shit, and a pot of negativity.

People who speak without thinking, who believe in their own little head that they are superior to others for no apparent reason disgust me. I unfortunately know some people who have failed to learn how communicate with themselves, and hence think it’s best to blabber all day long without making any sense. Don’t take me wrong here, I do not mind people who talk a lot! I rather love them, and enjoy their company. It’s those who instead of figuring out their own shit, and trying to resolve their own mess, would point fingers on you or if you ask even more, would taunt (for the lack of better word) you for nothing!

A sample of a conversation with one such person - 

MA (Mean Asshole) - Oh, You look so fit.. Are you dieting?
Me: No, I am not. I just eat clean, and exercise.
MA: What do you mean by eat clean?
Me: I don’t eat unhealthy stuff.
MA: How are you living then? I love eating.. I can’t live without KFC, I do not like people who don’t enjoy eating. What’s the purpose of life if you can’t even eat what you like.
Me: *Blank*

I am all in for people who love to eat, and don’t give a shit. But this! Come on lady! You were the one who asked me! And don’t even get me started on the conversation on how being a vegetarian is equivalent to living on grass for MAs. This was just an example of one of many conversations you have with MAs in daily life. If only - I knew how to block them from my zone.

Moving on…

I am not even going to address how it’s been another month since I last posted. In the latest news, we went to Italy last week. It was OH SO STUNNING! I don’t have enough words to describe how mesmerising is the beauty of this country. If a person has to choose one country from Europe, I would hands-down recommend Italy. It is THAT beautiful.

We flew to Venice, and returned to London from Rome. How we missed our morning flight to Venice is a whole different story, and deserves a separate blog post. For now, I will just mention that I have been relieved from the duties of planning a vacation for two of us. Not that I am complaining :D We covered Venice, Florence, Siena, Pissa, San Gimignano, Rome, and Vatican! 

Leaving you guys with pictures from Italy. Also, have any of you guys met any MA over the time? So let me know in the comment section.
