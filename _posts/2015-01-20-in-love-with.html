So, I am finished reading two books after The Lowland. I wanted to mention the first one as soon as it was done, but because the other one was the series of it, I thought of better wait for it. I have just completed the Cormoran Strike series, The Cuckoo’s Calling and The Silkworm, – two books written by Robert Galbraith (J.K. Rowling). If I were to write the review in one sentence, it would be – Drop everything, and go read these books!! These books are brilliantly written, well woven, and I was hooked till the last word.
Image Source - Google

Cormoran Strike series is a thriller mystery, based in London. It has two central characters, Cormoran Strike(the detective) and Robin (the assistant). I have been a JK Rowlingfan ever since Harry Potter happened. I admire her for opting for the pseudo name for these books. Her writing style is simple yet gripping. I would highly recommend these books to anyone who fancies reading something light and engaging. Good news is Rowling would be coming up with 5 more books in this series!! *Happy dance* 


P.S. I am done with three books out of 50 this year! Currently reading - Since I Don't Have You by Louise Candlish.
