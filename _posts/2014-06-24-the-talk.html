The last time I updated this space was more than a month back. These were the two most hectic months I have had in last 1 year. We had family visiting us for the first time ever since me moved to London, and oh boy, such a terrific time we had. I spent a whole 1 month with my in laws also for the first time EVER. To be honest, I was nervous. I hadn't ever stayed with them since I got married. But this one month changed everything in the relationship we share for good. I feel blessed when I write this that I have got the loveliest set of parents in law. They pampered me, spoiled me, and we had such an amazing time.

Also, Him and I got to behave like a tourist in our own city which was definitely the cherry on top. We had been postponing to visit to many places here just because of our sheer laziness. But now when mum and dad were here, we tied our laces and literally visited every nook and corner of London. This city never fails in making you fall in love it again and again.

As Samuel Johnson once said, "When a man is tired of London, he is tired of life; for there is in London all that life can afford."

Moving on, now as mum dad have gone back to India, we are slowly coming back to our daily routine. Summers have finally arrived here in London and the weather has never been so good as it has been lately. The pleasant weather has been ticking the travel bug in me but sadly we do not have any plans anytime soon. I have so much to talk about on this blog, so would be coming here more often in coming time.
