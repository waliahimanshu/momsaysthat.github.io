It was Diwali yesterday. As much as I hate to be away from my family around this time of the year, I also love how I get to experience the festivities of both sides of the world. You see, I have been in festive mode since the beginning of October - first with the Navratras, then the Dushera, Karva Chauth, and Diwali. Ideally, I would have calmed my horses by now but since I am in London - my excitement is still the same for Black Friday Sale, Christmas, Boxing Day, and the New Years. So yea, if you think of it, it's not that bad at all.

October was the month of cleaning and organising. In India, everybody begins to declutter their houses around this time of the year. So that's what I did. I began with asking for help from Him which clearly didn't go as per the plan.

Then came the rounds of Laundrette for curtain cleanings, and all that glamorous stuff. Not going to bore you with that. What I do want to share with everyone is a DIY that I did to organise my kitchen. Apologies, if I sound like a middle-class penny saver, but that's what I am. :P

I have been reusing emptied mason jars from pickles/ jams/ chutneys/ to put my spices in. What nagged my OCD to death was their lid covers. Yea, since they all had different lid covers, I couldn't deal with it anymore. Long story short, I spray painted the lids gold, and OH MY GOD! They look beautiful. (As petty as it may sound, I am in LOVE with gold spray paint).

Next was to label them. I had to label them because of Him because once I had a bad tummy ache, and my dear husband fetched me cumin seeds instead of Carom Seeds because clearly he couldn't see a difference. I got chalk board stickers, which are again REUSABLE, and you can wipe off the chalk paint from them easily.
